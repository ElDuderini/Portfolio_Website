An VR juggling game

Juggling Jestice is my senior project that I have worked on over the course of a couple of years. The premise of the game is that you are a contestant in competitive juggling. You face off against an AI opponent and compete with others on an online leaderboard to see who gets the best time. It includes code that I have had to redo as I have improved at my skills, with a lot of my original code still existing in the project. I feel like it is a great showcase of how I have gotten to improve as I progressed through my degree.
